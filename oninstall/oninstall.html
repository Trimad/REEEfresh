REEEfresh

Thank you for downloading my browser extension. Here is what I want you to know about it:

  • REEEfresh differs from other "refresh" apps by allowing you to refresh just your Salesforce cases instead of the whole page, reducing bandwidth usage and load times. Don't worry, you can still refresh the whole page if you want to!
  • REEEfresh is open source and under MIT License.
  • REEEfresh will always be free.
  • REEEfresh will never have ads.
  • REEEfresh will never track your browsing history, nor store, or use any of your personal data.
  • Do you like this browser extension? Buy me a coffee using one of the QR codes below!

Last updated August 4th, 2022

Dead simple controls. Click on the frog, enter how often you'd like to refresh the page in seconds and flip the switch. Keep It Simple, Stupid!

Want to know how long you've got left before the next refresh? The List View refresh button gets turned into a countdown timer!
